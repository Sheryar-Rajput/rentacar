About us

We are proud to be the first Rent-a-Car company that has started economy cars to make Pakistan tours for everyone. Our aim is to keep going with new innovations to boost Pakistan inbound and domestic tourism by giving the consumers wider range of choice to see the beautiful mountains and valleys of Pakistan.

As there is slump of foreign tourists to Pakistan we have launched special prices of tour packages that are available in our website of www.pakistantourism.com.pk but nevertheless if you have specific travel needs then do write us, our team of tourism professionals would design a tailor-made program to suit your tour requirements. We would always recommend you to hire a chauffeur-driven car that would give you peace of mind and more secure while driving in hilly areas of Pakistan. The cars would take you across Pakistan to explore the Himalaya, Karakoram, Silk Route and the most beautiful north Pakistan but one needs 4X4 drive vehicles for off-track valleys in the mountain region of Pakistan. For all kind of road transport services Pakistan Rent a Car would be the best choice.
